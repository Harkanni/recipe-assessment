A Classic Nigerian Jollof Rice

A rich, smoky, and delicious West African rice dish Perfect for parties, gatherings, or a simple comfort meal.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 mins
  • Cook Time: 45 mins
  • Servings: 4
  • Difficulty: Easy

Dish Image

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long-grain rice
  • 4 large tomatoes
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp thyme

Instructions

  1. Blend tomatoes, pepper, and onion into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil and fry the tomato paste until the raw taste reduces.
  3. Add seasonings and chicken stock, then bring to a boil.
  4. Pour in washed rice, reduce heat, and allow to cook.
  5. Stir occasionally until rice becomes soft and fluffy.

Tips

For a smoky flavor, allow the rice to slightly burn at the bottom. Also, use basmati rice for a softer texture.
